The Rules

How It Works

01

Format

Double elimination tournament played by doubles teams. Lose once and you're in the losers bracket — lose twice and you're watching from the sidelines with a snack.

02

Win Condition

First team to reach exactly 21 points wins the round. You must land on 21 — not over it.

03

The Bust Rule ⚠️

If your score exceeds 21 on your turn, your score resets to 11 points. Every bag counts — think before you throw!

04

Scoring

3 points for a bag through the hole. 1 point for a bag on the board. Cancellation scoring is used — points are netted between teams each frame.

05

Court Setup

Boards are placed 27 feet apart (hole to hole). Partners stand at opposite boards and alternate throws with the opposing team.

06

Spirit of the Game

Good sportsmanship is required. Friendly trash talk is encouraged. This is a Revelyst event — let's have fun and make some memories.

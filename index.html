/ THE RULES

How It
Works.

01
Scoring

3 pts through the hole · 1 pt on the board · Cancellation scoring applies each frame.

02
Win Condition

First team to reach exactly 21 wins the round. You must land on it — not blow past it.

03
The Bust Rule ⚠️

Exceed 21 and your score resets to 11. Think before you throw.

04
Court Setup

Boards 27 ft apart (hole to hole). Partners stand at opposite ends and alternate throws.

05
Spirit of the Game

Good sportsmanship required. Friendly trash talk encouraged. Make some memories.
